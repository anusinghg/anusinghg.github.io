Bookart

Webapp named as Bookart: Used book buying and selling system

Language of implementation: HTML, CSS, JS, PHP, MYSQL

The objective of this website is to provide a platform where the seller (students) can sell their used books. As this website is exclusively for books the users will be able to view books which might not be available on other website as it would get lost in the huge amount of products available. Moreover the buyer can meet the seller on the webapp itself through contact number.
